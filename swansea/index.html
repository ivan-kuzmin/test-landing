Swansea City AFC and Mercuryo

When two strong and ambitious players are united in one team, the result is a spectacular game.

We are looking forward to working with Mercuryo and welcome them into our Club Partnership portfolio for this current season. We are thrilled that Mercuryo chose to work with us for the 2020-21 season

Rebecca Edwards-Symmons

Swansea City’s head of commercial

Swansea City is pleased to unveil Mercuryo as the club’s first official cryptocurrency partner.

Mercuryo.io is a European cryptocurrency payment processing provider, with over 350,000 users and more than 150 crypto projects in its portfolio. In two years, the company has become one of the leading crypto-fiat gateways in Europe, offering both B2B and B2C services including cryptocurrency cards, multicurrency crypto wallets, business widgets, and crypto acquiring.

Mercuryo are an energetic and goal-driven team, who aim to bring innovation to the world of football, and share in the enthusiasm and sense of purpose of the Swansea City players and fans.

Our partnership with Swansea City rests upon similar values - determination, teamwork, and performance - and signifies the union of sports and technology.

Petr Kozyakov

Mercuryo's co-founder and CBDO
